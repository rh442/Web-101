What is Carbon Footprint?

Carbon footprint is the amount of greenhouse gases (carbon dioxide, methane, nitrous oxide, etc) that an individual produces. This can range from direct production such as burning fuel when driving a car or indirect production such as the gases emitted through the process of a package being delivered. The build up of these greenhouse gases can lead to increase in global temperature which we refer to as global warming.
